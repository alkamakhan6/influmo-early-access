About Influmo

We help creators build lean, pro teams so they can focus on the craft.

Our Mission

Content moves fast. Influmo makes it effortless for influencers to find and hire collaborators — editors, designers, page managers, scriptwriters — who fit their niche, budget, and timeline.

What We’re Building

  • Find Collabs: curated brand briefs with clear budgets and deadlines.
  • BYOT: build your own team of collaborators with verified profiles.
  • Messaging: simple request → chat → deliver flow.
