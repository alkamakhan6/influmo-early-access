About Influmo

Influmo is a visionary platform built to connect influencers with top-tier collaborators — including video editors, strategists, designers, and more. Our mission is to simplify the hiring process for content creators, enabling them to focus on what they do best: creating engaging, high-quality content.

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by Alkama Khan, Influmo began as an idea to solve a challenge faced by creators worldwide — finding reliable, skilled collaborators without wasting time and resources. Through community-driven testing and feedback, we are building a tool that works seamlessly for influencers, managers, and creative teams.

Leadership

Alkama Khan

Founder & CEO

Alkama Khan is a passionate entrepreneur and creative strategist dedicated to empowering content creators. With expertise in digital media, influencer marketing, and community growth, Alkama leads Influmo with a focus on innovation, collaboration, and delivering real-world impact for creators.

Our Mission

We aim to be the go-to hub for influencers to discover collaborators, manage projects, and grow their brand — all in one place. By leveraging technology and a creator-first approach, we are building a platform that is as dynamic and adaptable as the people who use it.
